Introduction¶

Dataset Description¶

This data set contains information about 10,000 movies collected from The Movie Database (TMDb), including user ratings and revenue.

Question(s) for Analysis¶

  • 1)Which Movie has (higher/lower)budget?
  • 2)Which Movie has (higher /lower )revenue ?
  • 3)Which Movie has (higher /lower )vote average?
  • 4)Which Movies have better vote Calssic or Modern ?
  • 5)How much the number of movie production changed over the years?
  • 6)What are the most common genres ?
  • 7)What is the most prefered run time ?
  • 8)What is the Best Month to releas a movie ?
